simple_travel_plan module

Suppose a passenger starts from x, and he/she plans to go y a SIMPLE travel plan might look like this: x -> bus stop 1: the passenger walks from x to bus stop 1 bus stop 1 -> bus stop 2 (by bus route xxxxx): the passenger takes bus xxxxx, from bus stop 1 to bus stop 2 bus stop 2 -> y: the passenger walks from bus stop 2 to destination y
